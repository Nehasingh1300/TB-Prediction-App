About



SmartTBX is a webapp that is used for diagnosis of Pulmonary Tuerbculosis using Image Processing and Deep Learning. The detection happens in two phases.In the first phase, the Xray Image is preprocessed with lung boundary reductiona and histogram equalization. The second phase consists of the prediction of tuberculosis The processed image is passed which gives the prediction for the Xray uploaded.
